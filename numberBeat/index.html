How to play?

Number Battle ⚔️ is a simple two-player strategy game built with JavaScript, where luck and decision-making go hand in hand. Players take turns generating a random number between 1 and 10. Each number (except 1) adds to the player’s current score for that turn. However, if a player generates a 1, they lose all the points accumulated in that round and their turn immediately ends. At any point during their turn, a player can choose to “Hold,” which safely adds their current score to their total score and passes the turn to the other player. The challenge lies in deciding when to stop and secure points, or continue generating numbers and risk losing everything. The first player to reach 50 total points wins the game, making every turn a balance between risk and strategy.
